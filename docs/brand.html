Usage Guidelines

Do: Use the mark on dark backgrounds. Maintain clear space around the logo. Link to recourseos.com when featuring the brand.

Don't: Stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo. Place on busy backgrounds. Use the mark as a generic "security" icon.
